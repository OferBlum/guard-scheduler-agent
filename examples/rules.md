# rules.md — Standing Rules

## Positions

| Position | Soldiers per shift |
|----------|--------------------|
| Operator | 1 |
| Shift Commander | 1 |

## Shifts

3 shifts per day, 8 hours each:
- 06:00–14:00
- 14:00–22:00
- 22:00–06:00

## Rest

- Minimum rest between shifts: 8 hours

## Shift Limit

- No weekly shift cap per soldier

## Rotation Cycles

- Number of rotations: 2
- Each weekend one rotation goes home ("home rotation") and the other stays on base ("base rotation")
- Update which rotation is on base before building each weekly schedule

## Weekend

- Weekend begins Thursday at 10:00 and ends Sunday at 14:00
- Soldiers in the home rotation cannot be assigned shifts during these hours
- **Base rotation rule:** the "base rotation" designation applies only to the defined weekend. After the weekend all soldiers return to full availability unless otherwise specified.
